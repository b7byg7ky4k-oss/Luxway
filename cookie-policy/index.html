Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how LuxWay Drivers may use cookies, local storage and similar technologies on this website.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored on your device. Similar technologies, such as local storage, can remember choices or support website functions.

Cookies and Technologies We May Use

  • Essential technologies used to operate the website, remember your cookie choice and support navigation.
  • Booking links such as WhatsApp and email links, which open third-party services outside this website.
  • Google Maps Places autocomplete if a Google Maps API key is configured.
  • Google Analytics and Meta Pixel if the tracking IDs are configured on the website.

Cookie Choice

The website shows a cookie notice and stores your choice in local storage as luxwayCookieChoice. You can clear this choice by deleting site data in your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Third-party providers may process technical data according to their own policies when their services are used or enabled. This may include WhatsApp, Google, Meta, email providers and hosting providers.

Managing Cookies

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Some features, such as saved preferences or third-party autocomplete, may work differently if cookies or storage are disabled.
