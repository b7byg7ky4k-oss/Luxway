Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how LuxWay Drivers handles information submitted through this website, WhatsApp, email and booking request forms.

Who We Are

LuxWay Drivers provides private NCC chauffeur and transfer services in Rome, Italy. For privacy questions, contact luxwaydrivers@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details, such as name, phone number, WhatsApp number and email address.
  • Booking details, such as pickup address, destination, date, time, flight number, cruise information, passengers, luggage and special requests.
  • Technical data that may be processed by browser, hosting, analytics, maps or third-party booking links.

How We Use Information

  • To answer requests and confirm availability.
  • To organize transfers, tours and chauffeur services.
  • To coordinate pickup details by WhatsApp or email.
  • To improve website performance, security and user experience.

Legal Basis

We process booking information to respond to pre-contractual requests and provide requested services. Where required, optional analytics or marketing technologies rely on consent.

Sharing Information

Information may be shared only when needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, operate the website, or use third-party services such as WhatsApp, email, hosting, Google Maps, Google Analytics or Meta Pixel if configured.

Data Retention

Booking and communication details are kept only as long as reasonably necessary for service coordination, accounting, legal obligations and customer support.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction or objection to processing where applicable. Send requests to luxwaydrivers@gmail.com.

Terms & Conditions

Booking requests sent through this website are not final confirmations until LuxWay Drivers confirms availability and details by WhatsApp or email. Prices, routes, waiting times, luggage capacity and service conditions may depend on the selected vehicle, itinerary and final confirmation.
